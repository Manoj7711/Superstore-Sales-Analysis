
# Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Superstore Sales Dataset** sourced from Kaggle and presents key business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, data transformation, exploratory analysis, KPI development, pivot table analysis, and dashboard creation.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often generate large volumes of sales data but struggle to extract actionable insights from it. This project aims to answer critical business questions such as:

* Which product categories generate the highest sales and profit?
* Which sub-categories are driving business growth?
* Which products or segments are causing losses?
* How do sales and profits vary across months and years?
* Which states contribute the most to revenue and profitability?

## Key Findings

### Technology is the Top Performing Category

Technology generated the highest sales and profit among all categories, making it the most valuable business segment.

### Phones Generate Maximum Sales

The Phones sub-category recorded the highest sales revenue, indicating strong customer demand.

### Copiers Generate Maximum Profit

Copiers emerged as the most profitable sub-category, contributing significantly to overall profit.

### Tables Are Loss-Making

The Tables sub-category generated negative profit, indicating pricing, discounting, or operational inefficiencies.

### California Leads in Performance

California recorded the highest sales and profit, making it the strongest-performing state.

### Consistent Yearly Growth

Both sales and profit showed steady growth from 2014 to 2017, reflecting positive business performance.

### Strong Seasonal Demand

November and December generated the highest sales and profit, suggesting increased demand during the holiday season.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Revenue Growth

* Focus marketing efforts on Technology products.
* Increase inventory for high-demand products such as Phones.

### Profitability Improvement

* Investigate losses in the Tables sub-category.
* Optimize pricing and discount strategies.

### Inventory Planning

* Maintain additional stock before peak seasons.
* Forecast demand based on historical trends.

### Regional Expansion

* Replicate successful sales strategies used in California across other regions.
